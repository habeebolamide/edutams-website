Stakeholders

We understand that school management is faced with challenges and varied expectations of the stakeholders. We identified the diverse needs of the education stakeholders and designed our solution to deliver on target value.

Ministry

The Ministry Staff are the Super Administrators on the OGSERA platform. They keep track of the activities of schools in every zones across the State without limitation.

Zeo

The Zonal Education Officers acts as representatives of the Ministry in their assigned zones and ensures that school activities are in order.

SPA

SPA is an additional role of a teacher in a school. He/she is responsible for the profiling of teachers students and parents of that school into the OGSERA platform.

TEACHER

The teacher in a school can assigned to teach a subject or multiple subjects and also handle a class as the class teacher.

STUDENT

The students can access their dossier on the OGSERA platform. This can be done through any internet enabled device.

PARENT

parents can get access to the performance of their wards via the platform. This can be done through any internet enabled device.
