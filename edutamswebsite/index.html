Our Unique Value

EduTAMS provide education stakeholders with increased, distinctive value and expandable
capabilities to respond to new requirements.
1

Personalization

We offer a completely new user experience that customizes a single solution for different schools in Africa and still enables them to be able to meet their own local peculiarities and needs.

2

Self-Boarding

A Do it Yourself (DIY) approach that offers new experience for administrators to effortlessly start and complete school set up process in simplified structured stages.

3

Optimised Platform

Lightweight technology with integrated modules to automate critical school procedures, as well as cross-platform data analytics to support decision making.

4

Agency Approach

We provide third-party agent with a personalized platform that provides TAMS as a service to schools in exchange for a commission.

5

Integrated Payment

Integrated bespoke payment gateway that connects bank channels in order to process and settle fees/payment collections at exceptionally cheap rates.
