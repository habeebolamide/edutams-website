Central EMIS

The Central EMIS provides functionalities for a State-wide and Zonal level control of key activities of approved Schools with a Dashboard to provide real-time data analytic on distribution of schools, teachers and students across the Classes and the Local Government Areas of the State.

Profile Management

The Profile Manager extends functionalities of the roles and permissions to create profile account and provide a level of access for Education Officers, Teachers, Learners and Parents. A unique Learner Identification Number (LIN) is generated and associated to each learner in public and private basic schools to track transfer history.

Enrollment Management

The Enrollment Manager has inter-connected functionalities to manage admission of new learners into Primary School category and transfer of Learners across Primary and Secondary Schools. It also automates the entire process of placing learners from public and private Primary Schools into the neighborhood Secondary Schools

Our strategic approach and experience are unique and groundbreaking in ogunstate with edutams powering over 1.5m learners, 10k schools, 30k teachers in 12 thousand schools - primary and secondary schools, and providing real-time data and Analytics to Ogun State Government - OGSG to support policy formulation, monitoring, and regulation in the education sector.
